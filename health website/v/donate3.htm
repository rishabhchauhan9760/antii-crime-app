Benefits

Benefits to the Doner

The Doner gets a letter of Appreciation from us.
Other than this the doner gets a feeling of Satisfaction for Donation.
Helping others for a cause Gives you a great Happiness which cannot be given from Money.

Benefits to the takers

They get their need of medicine fulfilled for free or for low cost sometime.
If patients need for medicine is very urgent we deliver medicine at their doorstep.

Other benefits

If we Recycle the unused medicine then we the the demand of that medicine can be fulfiled by the stock of medicines we have collected , which will then lead to decrease in demand of production of new medicines, which will hence lead to decrease in rates of medicines.
