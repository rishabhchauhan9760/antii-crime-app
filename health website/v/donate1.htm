What are our Services ?


Our platform connects surplus drugs from various pharmacies and stores with clinics and patients

We collect unexpired unused drugs from clinics, pharmacies , other patients and provide them to the low income patients.

In this way a medicine which is of no use to you becomes a lifesaver for someone else.

We have doorstep Pickup from the donator, The needy patients can get the medicines from the clinics.

The medicines collected by us is also used for health camps in various areas.
