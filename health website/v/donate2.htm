How we work?

Our Services are available over our app. The user needs to download our App.
There are following FOUR steps for donation of medicines:

Step 1

Firstly the user needs to sign in our App.

Step 2

Then User needs to put the details of the medicines which He/She wants to donate.
Details like medicine Name,Manufacturing Date,Expiry Date, etc.

Step 3

Now our work starts from here.
We will have the details of all doners and once in two weeks we will sent our volunteers to collect the unused medicines form your Doorstep.

Step 4

Now we will send the collected medicines to various NGOs, stores from where it can be given to low income needy patients or we can also make our collected medicines reach to various health camps.
